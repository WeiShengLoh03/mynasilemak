Nasi Lemak

Introduction

Nasi Lemak – This iconic Malaysian dish is all about that fragrant coconut milk rice, topped with crispy anchovies, spicy sambal, hard-boiled eggs, roasted peanuts, and fresh cucumber .

Recipes

Coconut Rice

  • 2 cups of jasmine rice
  • 1½ cups coconut milk
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 pandan leaf, tied into a knot
  • 1 tsp salt

Sambal Spices

  1. 10 dried chilies, soaked in warm water
  2. 3 fresh red chilies
  3. 3 shallots
  4. 2 cloves garlic
  5. 1 tsp belacan (shrimp paste)
  6. 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  7. 1 tbsp sugar (palm sugar or brown sugar preferred)
  8. Salt to taste
  9. 1 onion, sliced thin (for caramelizing)
